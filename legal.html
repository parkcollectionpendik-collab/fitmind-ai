Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 16, 2026 · Türkçe özet aşağıda mevcuttur.
TL;DR: We collect only what we need to provide the service. We never sell your data. You can delete your account and all data at any time.

1. Who We Are

FitMind AI ("we", "us", "our") operates the FitMind AI fitness platform at fitmindai.ai. We are committed to protecting your personal information.

2. What Data We Collect

Account Information

  • Name and email address (required for account creation)
  • Password (stored encrypted, never readable by us)

Profile & Health Data

  • Age, height, weight, gender (optional, used to personalize your plan)
  • Fitness goals and equipment preferences
  • Progress logs (weight measurements, body measurements)
  • Workout completion records

Usage Data

  • Pages visited, features used (for improving the product)
  • Device type and browser (for technical compatibility)

Payment Data

Payments are processed by Paddle. We never store your credit card information. Paddle's privacy policy applies to payment data.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and personalize the FitMind AI service
  • To generate AI-powered fitness and nutrition plans
  • To send service-related emails (welcome, reminders)
  • To improve the product based on usage patterns
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. AI & Your Data

Your profile data (goals, fitness level, weight) is sent to Anthropic's Claude AI to generate personalized plans. This data is processed according to Anthropic's Privacy Policy. We do not use your data to train AI models.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Supabase — database hosting (EU/US servers)
  • Anthropic — AI plan generation
  • Paddle — payment processing
  • Resend — email delivery

If you connect with a physiotherapist, your profile data (goals, progress) is visible to your assigned physiotherapist.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by emailing privacy@fitmindai.ai. We will delete your data within 30 days.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Delete your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data
  • Objection: Object to certain processing

8. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for authentication (Supabase session cookies). We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

9. Security

Your data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt. We follow industry-standard security practices.

10. Children

FitMind AI is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes

We may update this policy. We will notify you by email for significant changes.

📧 Privacy Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email us at privacy@fitmindai.ai
